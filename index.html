Eddy is the first heal nano in the game. Heal is one of the most important powers in the game and is recomended to have a heal nano equiped at all times.

Peach Creek Estates - Peach Creek Commons (The Future)

Fusions:

Fusion Eduardo

Nanos Gained:

Eduardo

Recamended Nano(s): Eddy (Heal)

Peach Creek Estates does not have any monsters in it, but however it has a ton of fusion matter on the ground that hurts you when you walk over it. Since it is still a low level area, Eddy is recomended if you do take damage from the fusion matter if you have his Heal ability. The cannons there are a good way to get to the Fusion Edwardo Fusion Portal which is on a elevated landscape instead of doing the platforming. Edwardos abilities are sort of mid-teir. He does have a Group Heal ability, but if you have Eddy already with Heal, Eduardo's Drain (Damage Over Time) would be best since it would be your first damage ability,

Polky Oaks Junior High is the same as the Future version. It has the more advanced platforming with spining platforms and moving ones. Fusions:

 

Fusion Dee Dee   Fusion Dexter (Secret Lair)

Nanos Gained:

Dee Dee

Recamended Nano(s): Blossom (Antidote), Wilt (Jump)

Mandarks House is one of the zones that have the most upward platforming. Most of the ground has Fusion matter, which Blossom would come in handy. Reactor Works - Power Plant

Fusions:

   

Fusion Professor Utonium   Fusion Professor Utonium (Secret Lair)   Fusion Fuzzy Lupkins (Secret Lair)

Nanos Gained:

Professor Utonium

Recomended Nanos: Eddy (Heal), Edwardo (Drain), Wilt (Jump), Dee Dee-Billy (Damage) Numbuh Five (Leech), Megas (Sneak), Prof Utonium (Rocket)

The Power Plant is one of the tallist and biggest zones in the game. About 50% of the zone is based on very high platforming (above ground) and the other half being a ton of grouped monsters on the ground. There is a pathway floating in the sky from the radio tower to the power plant chimneyis a good way to get around, if you can make it to the top. If you ahve Edd as a Guide, Billy gives you alot of missions that tell u to go up to that part of the area. This pathway also leads you to the end of the zone to the exit portal to leave the area. as for the Professor Utonium nano, he is by far one of the best mobility nano in the game. He has a group Jump which is good if you are playign with friends, but the power that makes Utonium one of the best is his Rocket Ability. Rocket basicly gives you a double jump that is really helpful for any platforming part of the game.

Nanos Gained:

Him

recomended Nanos: Eddy-Him (Heal), Blosssom (Antidote), Wilt (Jump), Professor Utonium (Rocket)

Fusion Scotsman   Fusion Scotsman (Secret Lair)

Nanos Gained:

Demongo

Recomended Nanos: Blossom (Antidote), Wilt-Bubbles (Jump), Professor Utonuim (Rocket)

The Entirety of Leaky Lake is the zone itself. The whole zone is primarily made up high platforming and zip lines. The pathway goes around the giant rock in the center of the lake and when you do get to the top, you take the zipline down to get ot the exit portal. The Demongo nano is a interesting one since he is both offense and support. Damage beign offense and Revive being support. Nanos Gained:

Four Arms

Recomended Nanos: Wilt-Bubbles (Jump), Professor Utonium (Rocket)

The Fissure is one of the only Infected Zones that is underground. There are no monsters in this area so damage nanos are not needed. The area consist of 3 raised levels all 3 being diffrent hights. This is where nore advnaced platforming is preformed with moving platforms, raising platforms, and a lot of siplines. There are also glowing Green Balls that move across some of the platforms that can knock you off them. Any Nano with Jump or Rocket is HIGHLY recomended for this area. Same as Numbuh Five, Fourarms had the Leech ability which is by far one of the most powerfull moves in the game. Fourarms also has a EXP bonus aility which is good if you are in a dead level (no more missions for that said level).

Recomended Nanos: Wilt-Bubbles (Jump), Professor Utonuim (Rocket)
